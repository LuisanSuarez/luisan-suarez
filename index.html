Meet Luisan

I was really lucky when I was a kid. More than just an engineer, my dad was a nerd. Meaning that every time I asked how something worked (he must have enjoyed that phase where children ask “why” about everything), I didn’t get annoyed deflections: I got discussions. For better or worse, I got used to my curiosity getting answers. When dad stopped having answers, I started figuring them out myself. Almost two decades later, I’m still convinced getting answers is just about knowing how (and being willing) to look.

Fast forward: I'm spending my days working out, eating well (fingers crossed I’ll make it to 150 years old!), and learning as much as I can. Most of the technical focus gets directed at JavaScript and/or Python, but I like to read wide as well: physics, philosophy, politics, and economics are few of the things I read up on my free time. I just finished the Full Stack Web Development course at Lambda School. Built a few nice projects during that time, as well as taking time off from the course to give Computer Science lectures to new Lambda cohorts.
